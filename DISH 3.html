PINAKBET

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup bagoong (fermented shrimp paste)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 small bitter melon (ampalaya), sliced
  • 1 cup string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 medium eggplant, sliced
  • 1 medium squash (kalabasa), peeled and cubed
  • 1 small okra, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, saut√© garlic, onion, and tomato in a little oil until softened and fragrant.

2. Add the pork belly and cook until it turns golden brown.

3. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Lower the heat and let it simmer until the pork becomes tender.

4. Add the bagoong, and stir in the vegetables: bitter melon, string beans, eggplant, squash, and okra. Season with salt and pepper.

5. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have blended together.

6. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
