KARE-KARE

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb oxtail, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 lb tripes, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 lb beef short ribs (optional)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup ground rice (or rice flour)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 bunch banana hearts, sliced
  • 1 bunch bok choy (pechay) or other greens
  • 1/2 cup shrimp paste (bagoong), for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water or beef broth for boiling

Instructions:

1. In a large pot, bring water or beef broth to a boil. Add oxtail, tripes, and beef short ribs (if using). Let it cook until the meat is tender, about 1.5-2 hours.

2. While the meat is cooking, sauté garlic and onion in a separate pan until fragrant and softened.

3. Once the meat is tender, add the sautéed garlic and onion to the pot along with peanut butter and ground rice. Stir until the peanut butter is dissolved, and the sauce thickens.

4. Add the sliced eggplants, banana hearts, and greens (bok choy or pechay) to the pot. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the flavor of the sauce as needed.

6. Serve the Kare-Kare hot, accompanied with shrimp paste (bagoong) on the side. Enjoy!
