SISIG

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly or pork shoulder, boiled and chopped
  • 1/2 lb pork liver, boiled and chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 Thai bird’s eye chilies (optional, for spice), chopped
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 egg (optional, for topping)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil for sautéing
  • Chopped green onions for garnish
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise (optional, for creamy sisig)

Instructions:

1. In a large pan, heat vegetable oil and sauté the garlic and onion until fragrant and softened.

2. Add the boiled pork belly and pork liver to the pan, and stir-fry until slightly crispy.

3. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, and calamansi juice, and stir well to combine. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.

4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Add the chopped bird’s eye chilies (if using) for extra heat.

5. If you're making creamy sisig, stir in the mayonnaise at this point.

6. Transfer the sisig to a hot sizzling plate, and top with a raw egg (if using). Let it cook for a minute or two to cook the egg.

7. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve immediately with steamed rice or as an appetizer!
