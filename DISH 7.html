KINILAW

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fresh tuna or any firm fish, cut into cubes
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 3-4 pieces of finger chili (siling labuyo), chopped (optional, for heat)
  • 1/2 cup vinegar (preferably coconut vinegar)
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice (or lemon juice as an alternative)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, combine the fresh tuna, red onion, tomatoes, cucumber, and chili peppers (if using).

2. Add the vinegar, calamansi juice, grated ginger, and fish sauce to the bowl. Toss everything gently to combine.

3. Season with salt and pepper to taste. If you prefer a stronger tang, you can add more vinegar or calamansi juice.

4. Let the mixture sit for about 10-15 minutes to marinate and allow the flavors to meld together.

5. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving, if desired.

6. Serve chilled and enjoy this refreshing Filipino dish with steamed rice or as an appetizer!
