LUMPIA

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork (or chicken, shrimp, or beef)
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pack lumpia wrappers (spring roll wrappers)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

1. In a large pan, saut√© garlic and onion until softened.

2. Add the ground pork (or your choice of protein) and cook until browned.

3. Add the shredded carrots and cabbage, and cook for 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.

4. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and ground black pepper. Let it cool to room temperature.

5. Once the mixture has cooled, take one lumpia wrapper and spoon about 2 tablespoons of the mixture into the center.

6. Roll the wrapper tightly, folding in the sides as you go, and seal the edge with a little water.

7. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the lumpia in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-5 minutes.

8. Drain on paper towels, then serve with sweet chili sauce for dipping. Enjoy!
