Lunch

Adobo

  • Pork (usually belly or chicken)
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay leaves
  • Peppercorn

Sinigang

  • Pork (or shrimp, fish)
  • Sinigang mix or tamarind
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Water spinach (kangkong)
  • Radish, eggplant, okra
  • Fish sauce

Kare-Kare

  • Oxtail or beef tripe
  • Peanut butter or ground peanuts
  • Annatto (achuete)
  • Bok choy, eggplant, string beans
  • Garlic and onions
  • Shrimp paste (bagoong)

Bicol Express

  • Pork
  • Coconut milk
  • Chilies
  • Shrimp paste
  • Garlic and onions
  • Ginger (optional)

Laing

  • Dried taro leaves
  • Coconut milk and cream
  • Dried fish or shrimp
  • Ginger
  • Garlic and onions
  • Chilies

Pancit Canton

  • Egg noodles
  • Chicken or pork strips
  • Cabbage, carrots, green beans
  • Soy sauce and oyster sauce
  • Garlic and onions
  • Calamansi (optional)

Tinola

  • Chicken (preferably with bones)
  • Green papaya or chayote
  • Malunggay or chili leaves
  • Ginger
  • Fish sauce
  • Garlic and onions

Lumpia

  • Ground pork or beef
  • Carrots and onions
  • Garlic
  • Lumpia wrapper
  • Soy sauce
  • Egg (for sealing)

Pinakbet

  • Eggplant, okra, squash, bitter melon
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic and onions
  • Shrimp paste
  • Pork belly (optional)

Bangus (Daing)

  • Milkfish (butterflied)
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil (for frying)
