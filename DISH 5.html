BOLA-BOLA

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1/4 lb shrimp, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup carrots, finely grated
  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground pork, shrimp, grated carrots, green onions, garlic, and onion.

2. Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, egg, ground black pepper, and salt. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly incorporated.

3. Gradually add breadcrumbs until the mixture holds together when shaped into a ball. You can add more breadcrumbs if needed.

4. Shape the mixture into small meatballs, about 1 inch in diameter.

5. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the meatballs in batches until they are golden brown and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes per batch. Make sure to turn them occasionally for even cooking.

6. Drain the cooked meatballs on paper towels to remove excess oil.

7. Serve hot with sweet and sour sauce or your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy!
